Unity-chan Puzzle Park (UCPP)

Enjoy match-3 puzzles with Unity-chan!

You can easily play with just your touch operations.

Swipe to move gems, match 3 gems of same color in line to clear the gems.

The objective of the game is to clear the mission set on the stage.

There are three modes in this game.

  • Practice mode: learn basic rules of the puzzle.
  • Quest mode: conquest various multi-stage quests.
  • Marathon mode: challenge your skill in endless stages.

You can choose a character from "Unity-chan!" universe who plays the puzzle together.

The characters can use their individual skills and items to support solving puzzles.

Complete puzzle stages, get Unity coins, and unlock characters.

This game supports Japanese and English language.

Character voices are available only in Japanese.

Things that are "in" this game. (Features)

  • You can play anytime and as many times as you like. (No waiting time for life recovery.)
  • Items are replenished every time you start a quest. (No need to buy and stock items.)
  • You can unlock all characters only with enough Unity coins.
  • Tutorial (static explanatory text and image only)
  • Advertising videos (rewarded ads and interstitial ads)

Things that are "not in" this game. (Restrictions)

  • In-game purchases (paid items, etc.)
  • Root boxes (Regardless of paid or free.)
  • Online feature (competition, cooperation, etc.)
  • Social communication (friends, rankings, etc.)
  • Cloud backend (save data storage, achievements, etc.)
